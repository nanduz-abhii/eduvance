Welcome to EduVance

A Next-Generation Campus Grading and Management Ecosystem powered by Artificial Intelligence.

Features

Everything You Need for Modern Education

EduVance brings advanced AI technologies directly into classrooms, enabling automated evaluation, seamless communication, and intuitive progress tracking.

AI-Powered Evaluation

Utilize cutting-edge Gemini LLM to automatically parse, transcribe, and grade hand-written assignments and essays based on semantic relevance.

Automated OMR Processing

Upload multi-choice question sheets. EduVance automatically extracts answers using Computer Vision, maps them against verified AI keys, and calculates scores instantaneously.

Real-Time Data Processing

Enjoy asynchronous processing. Submissions are processed in the background with a seamless user interface that auto-updates live as AI results finish.

Internal Marks System

Teachers can efficiently enter marks, and students have a tailored dashboard to monitor their progress, grades, and ongoing performance throughout the semester.

Cloud Attendance

Track attendance electronically. Record daily presence per student and subject, and easily fetch reports based on dates and departments.

Role-Based Access

Specific dashboards for Admins, Teachers, and Students. Clean approval workflows for educators, and individualized hubs for student materials.

EduVance

A digital platform that streamlines student enrollment, course management, grades tracking, and communication between students, faculty, and administration, improving efficiency and organization.
